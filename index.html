afup Logos & Identity Guidelines

This page presents the guidelines around afup's identity and its logos.

Introduction

The afup's logo was designed by Gaëtan Ark from Les Polypodes.

It conveys the notion of a community made of different communities, local groups, people, volunteers… All around a cube representing the basic element of construction.

Reference Documents

Here are the documents provided by Gaëtan. In case of any doubt, they are the ones to use as a reference.

  • afup-logos-reference-CMYK.ai
  • afup-logos-CMYK(editable).ai
  • afup-logos-RGB(editable).ai

Fonts

When working on these logos, the following fonts have to installed on your system:

  • Roboto from Google Font
  • Belgrano from Google Font
